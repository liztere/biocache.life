about

We live in an age with access to an unprecedented amount of information, but also an unprecedented amount of distractions. In an effort to counter the nauseating mess that is now the internet, I've put together--initially intended for myself--a simple site containing a collection of helpful tools that may help you navigate the struggles we face as humans, learning resources for a number of subjects, as well as links to pressing matters. My aim is to help foster the love for learning that we all innately have and to provide these resources in an intuitive navigatable manner. More importantly, I hope some of the content here may instill a sense of urgency towards developing compassion to ourselves and others and a will to stand against the psyops of our generation.read more.
