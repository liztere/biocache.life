about

We live in an age with access to an unprecedented amount of information, but also an unprecedented amount of distractions. On the site will be a collection of helpful tools that may help you navigate the struggles we face as humans, learning resources for a number of subjects, as well as links to pressing matters unique to our generation. My aim is to help foster the love for learning that we all innately have and to provide these resources in an intuitive navigatable manner. More importantly, I hope some of the content here may instill a sense of urgency towards developing compassion to ourselves and others.read more.
