By providing easy access to information in a neutral, user friendly way, we hope to spark passion for knowledge and a desire for greater autonomy in as many individuals as possible; something our school systems have failed to encourage.
The internet is an incredible place to learn a plethora of subjects for skill learning and self discovery. However, it has a steep learning curve in knowing how to navigate through the [bullshit] and other distractions; this discourages many novice internet users from exploring anything past social media and political discourse. Here at biocache.life, we hope to shine light on at least a fraction of the hidden gems on the internet, which is why we welcome anyone to suggest knowledge of elusive, intellectually enriching content pertaining to any subject to be added to the site.
